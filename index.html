How it works
Four steps.
Fully private.
01
Lock Assets
Connect your Miden wallet. Choose assets to lock into a private vault contract. Amounts stay hidden from everyone.
1 transaction
02
Set Conditions
Define your rule privately. Time-based, activity-based, or goal-based. Recipient wallets sealed inside the contract.
sealed note
03
Chain Watches
Miden contract monitors conditions silently. You check in regularly. Nobody outside sees the vault or its contents.
recurring txns
04
Auto Execute
Conditions met - assets release automatically via private Notes to recipient wallets. No manual step. No middleman.
automatic
Use cases
Built for real
crypto scenarios.
The Traveller
Going offline for months? Set a rule - if you do not check in, assets move to your cold wallet automatically. No one monitors it for you.
Time Locked Transfer
Lock assets with a time condition. Releases exactly when you set it. Private, automatic, trustless.
Deal Escrow
Two parties in a deal. Lock funds in a Privex vault. Both confirm - funds release. No trust needed. No lawyer.
Goal Based Release
Lock a grant or bounty. Releases automatically when a specific onchain action is verified. No committee needed.
Recurring Transfers
Pay contributors automatically on a schedule. Nobody sees the amounts or the relationship. Fully private.
Backup Wallet Protection
If your main wallet goes quiet, automatically shift holdings to your backup. Private safety net with zero exposure.
Why Miden
This only works
on Miden.

Private by default

Every vault rule, asset amount, and recipient is sealed inside a Miden Note. The chain stores only a cryptographic commitment - not the data.

Client side execution

Transactions execute locally on your device. ZK proof sent to chain. Nobody sees your vault logic, not even the validators.

Quantum resistant

Built with post-quantum cryptography. Your vault rules stay safe even as quantum computing advances.

Ethereum secured

Settled on Ethereum via Agglayer validity proofs. Miden privacy with Ethereum security underneath.
